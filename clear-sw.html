Use this page to clear broken service workers and caches.

Click "Check Status" to see current service workers.

Manual Steps (if buttons don't work):

  1. Open DevTools (F12)
  2. Go to "Application" tab
  3. Click "Service Workers" in left sidebar
  4. Click "Unregister" for all EventCall service workers
  5. Go to "Storage" in left sidebar
  6. Click "Clear site data"
  7. Close this tab and open EventCall fresh

Console Commands:

  • window.unregisterServiceWorkers() - Unregister all service workers
  • window.clearAppCache() - Clear caches and reload
